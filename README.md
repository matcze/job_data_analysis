# 📊 Job Market Analysis – Focus on Poland

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of the job market with a particular focus on **Poland**.  
The goal of the analysis is to explore trends in job postings, salary distributions, required skills, and geographic demand.

Using Python-based data analysis tools, the project investigates patterns in job offers and provides insights into the structure of the labor market.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals
The main objectives of this project are to:

- Demonstrate practical **data analysis skills**
- Practice **economic and labor market data visualization**
- Explore **global job market trends and inequalities**
- Present complex datasets in a **clear and accessible way**

---

## 🔍 Scope of Analysis
The project analyzes several key aspects of the job market:

- Number of specific job positions in relation to **global data**
- Number of job offers in **particular countries**
- Analysis of **cities in Poland** with the highest number of job offers
- Identification of **companies with the most job postings in Poland**
- Monthly distribution of job offers in Poland
- Analysis of job features such as:
  - Remote work availability
  - Degree requirements
- **Salary distribution** across different job positions
- **Most in-demand skills** depending on salary level and market demand

---

| Library | Purpose |

## 📈 Visualization Approach
Due to the large number of charts generated in this project, each visualization includes a **short conclusion placed directly below the graph**.

This approach helps to:

- Improve readability
- Keep interpretations close to the data
- Maintain clarity throughout the analysis
